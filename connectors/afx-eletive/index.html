Eletive

March 3, 2024 ¬∑ 37 min read
Pradeep Kadambar
Creator

Eletive is a People Success Platform that gathers real-time employee engagement surveys, performance management, OKR goal-setting, 360-feedback, and 1:1s in one intuitive and user-friendly tool. Eletive automates and streamlines tedious admin tasks, so HR, managers, and team members can focus on the business goals.


Connector Settings‚Äã

Steps

  1. Login to RSA Identity G&L console as System Administrator.
  2. Navigate to AFX > Connectors.
  3. Click on Create Connector and select the RESTful Web Service connector template.
  4. Enter the details as shown below under General tab.

  5. Click on the Settings tab and enter the required connection details.

  6. Click on Add More Parameters.
  7. Enter the details for APIKey as shown below and click OK.

  8. Provide the API key in the API Key field and click OK.
