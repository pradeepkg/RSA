Microsoft Dynamics 365

February 22, 2024 ¬∑ 91 min read
Pradeep Kadambar
Creator
Hany Mohamed
Contributor

Microsoft Dynamics 365 is a comprehensive cloud-based business application suite integrating ERP, CRM, and AI-driven insights. It streamlines operations, enhances customer engagement, and provides data-driven decision-making for businesses of all sizes, fostering growth and efficiency.

Versions: 7.5.2

Overview‚Äã

In an earlier blog, Microsoft Dynamics 365 Collections we looked into collecting accounts and entitlements from Microsoft Dynamics 365. In this article, we will discuss provisioning into Microsoft Dynamics 365 using REST APIs.

Connector Settings‚Äã

Steps

  1. Login to RSA Identity G&L console as System Administrator.
  2. Navigate to AFX > Connectors.
  3. Click on Create Connector and select the RESTful Web Service connector template.
  4. Enter the details as shown below under General tab.

  5. Click on the Settings tab and enter the required connection details.

  6. Click on Add More Parameters.
  7. Enter the details for scope as shown below and click OK.

  8. Provide the https://instance.dynamics.com/.default offline_access in the Scope field.
  9. Click on Get OAuth 2.0 Access Token and authorize.
  10. Click OK.
