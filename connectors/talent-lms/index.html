TalentLMS

May 2, 2024 · 47 min read
Pradeep Kadambar
Creator
TalentLMS is a user-friendly learning management system designed for businesses and educational institutions. With intuitive course creation tools, customizable learning paths, and robust tracking features, it enables organizations to deliver effective training to employees, customers, and partners. Its cloud-based platform offers scalability, ease of use, and comprehensive reporting, making it an ideal solution for modern training needs.

Versions: 8.0.0 P2

Overview​

It is recommended that you review the TalentLMS Collections guide before proceeding with this blog.

Connector Settings​

Steps

  1. Login to RSA Identity G&L console as System Administrator.
  2. Navigate to AFX > Connectors.
  3. Click on Create Connector and select the RESTful Web Service connector template.
  4. Enter the details as shown below under General tab.

  5. Click on the Settings tab and enter the required connection details.

  6. Click OK.

Supported Verbs​

Create an Account​

Steps

  1. Edit the connector and navigate to Capabilities tab.
  2. Click on Create an Account and configure as shown below.
