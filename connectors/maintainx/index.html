MaintainX

July 6, 2024 ¬∑ 44 min read
Pradeep Kadambar
Creator
MaintainX is a versatile maintenance management platform designed to streamline operations for businesses across various industries. With intuitive features for work orders, asset management, and team collaboration, MaintainX empowers organizations to efficiently track and manage maintenance tasks, enhance productivity, and ensure equipment reliability. Its user-friendly interface and mobile accessibility make it a convenient solution for modern maintenance needs.

Connector Settings‚Äã

Steps

  1. Login to RSA Identity G&L console as System Administrator.
  2. Navigate to AFX > Connectors.
  3. Click on Create Connector and select the RESTful Web Service connector template.
  4. Enter the details as shown below under General tab.

  5. Click on the Settings tab and enter the required connection details.

  6. Click on Add More Parameters.
  7. Enter the details for APIKey as shown below and click OK.

  8. Provide the API key in the API Key field and click OK.
