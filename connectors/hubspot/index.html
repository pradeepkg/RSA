HubSpot

May 14, 2024 · 38 min read
Pradeep Kadambar
Creator

HubSpot is a leading CRM platform that offers marketing, sales, and customer service tools to help businesses grow. It provides solutions for email marketing, lead generation, social media management, and more, all integrated into one platform. HubSpot's user-friendly interface and robust analytics make it a popular choice for companies seeking to streamline their customer relationship management efforts.

Versions: 8.0.0 P2

Overview​

It is recommended that you review the HubSpot Collections guide before proceeding with this blog.

Connector Settings​

Steps

  1. Login to RSA Identity G&L console as System Administrator.
  2. Navigate to AFX > Connectors.
  3. Click on Create Connector and select the RESTful Web Service connector template.
  4. Enter the details as shown below under General tab.

  5. Click on the Settings tab and enter the required connection details.

  6. Click on Add More Parameters.
  7. Enter the details for APIKey as shown below and click OK.

  8. Provide the API key in the API Key field and click OK.

    .

API Reference: https://developers.hubspot.com/docs/api/settings/user-provisioning

Steps
