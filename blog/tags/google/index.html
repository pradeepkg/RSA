Google Workspace

October 1, 2024 · 50 min read
Pradeep Kadambar
Creator

Google Workspace (formerly Google Suite) is a cloud-based suite of productivity tools that includes Gmail, Google Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides, and more. It facilitates seamless collaboration and communication among teams, offering real-time editing, sharing, and storage of documents. Designed for business efficiency, it enhances productivity and fosters collaboration by providing a unified platform for work-related tasks.

Versions: 8.0.0 P1 8.0.0 P4
