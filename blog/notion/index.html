Notion

April 18, 2024 ¬∑ 26 min read
Pradeep Kadambar
Creator
Notion is a versatile productivity tool offering note-taking, project management, and collaboration features. Its customizable workspace allows users to organize tasks, documents, and databases efficiently. With seamless integration across platforms, Notion enhances team collaboration and individual productivity, making it a popular choice for personal and professional use.

Versions: 8.0.0 P2

Account Collector‚Äã

This section explains the process for configuring an account collector for the Notion application. We will use the RSA G&L Generic REST collector üìò for this purpose.

Steps

  1. Login to RSA G&L console as System Administrator.
  2. Navigate to Resources > Applications.
  3. Click on Notion.
  4. Navigate to Collectors tab and click on Create Account Collector. Enter the details as shown below.

  5. Click Next and enter the connection details.



  6. Click Next. Select the data types that the collector will be collecting from Notion.

  7. Click Next. On the Account Data page, enter the following configurations.

Application Setup‚Äã

This section explains the process of setting up Notion for integration with G&L.

Steps

  1. Login to Notion https://www.notion.so/.
  2. Click on Settings and members.

  3. Click on Identity & provisioning under Integrations.

  4. Click on Add token.
  5. Copy the token and retain for use with G&L.
