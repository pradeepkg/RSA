HubSpot

April 29, 2024 · 32 min read
Pradeep Kadambar
Creator
HubSpot is a leading CRM platform that offers marketing, sales, and customer service tools to help businesses grow. It provides solutions for email marketing, lead generation, social media management, and more, all integrated into one platform. HubSpot's user-friendly interface and robust analytics make it a popular choice for companies seeking to streamline their customer relationship management efforts.

Versions: 8.0.0 P2

Identities ❌ | Accounts ✔️ | Groups ✔️ | App Roles ❌ | Entitlements ❌

Application​

Steps

  1. Login to RSA G&L console as System Administrator.
  2. Navigate to Resources > Application.
  3. Click on Create Application and select Other Application.
  4. Enter the details as shown below.
  5. Click Finish.

Account Collector​

This section explains the process for configuring an account collector for the HubSpot application. We will use the RSA G&L Generic REST collector 📘 for this purpose.

Steps

  1. Login to RSA G&L console as System Administrator.
  2. Navigate to Resources > Applications.
  3. Click on HubSpot.
  4. Navigate to Collectors tab and click on Create Account Collector. Enter the details as shown below.

  5. Click Next and enter the connection details.



  6. Click Next. Select the data types that the collector will be collecting from HubSpot

  7. Click Next. On the Account Data page, enter the following configurations.


Request
MethodGET
URL/crm/v3/owners (Sample Response)
Request Headers
Acceptapplication/json
AuthorizationBearer ${AuthenticationToken}
Response Mappings
AccountId$.results[*].email
Id$.results[*].id
  1. Click Next. On the Group Data page, enter the following configurations.


Request
MethodGET
URL/settings/v3/users/teams (Sample Response)
Request Headers
Acceptapplication/json
AuthorizationBearer ${AuthenticationToken}
Response Mappings
GroupId$.results.*.name
Id$.results.*.id
  1. Click Next. On the Account Group Membership page, enter the following configurations.


Request
MethodGET
URL/crm/v3/owners (Sample Response)
Request Headers
Acceptapplication/json
AuthorizationBearer ${AuthenticationToken}
Response Mappings
Account Identifier$.results[*].id
Group Identifier$.results[*].teams[*].id
  1. Click Next. In the Map Collector Attributes to Account Attributes, map the collected account attributes.

  2. Click Next. In the Map Collector Attributes to Account Mapping Attributes, map the user reference attribute.

  3. Click Next. In the Map Collector Attributes to Group Attributes, map the collected group attributes.

  4. Click Next. In the User Resolution Rules, map the user to account.

  5. Click Next. In the Member Account Resolution Rules, map the account.

  6. Click Finish. Use the Test function to make sure the configurations are accurate.

Application Setup​

This section explains the process of setting up HubSpot for integration with G&L.

Steps

  1. Login to HubSpot https://app.hubspot.com/.
  2. On the right hand corner, click on Profile & Preferences.

  3. Click on Private apps under Integrations.
  4. Click on Create a private app.

  5. Provide a suitable application name.

  6. Click on Scopes tab and provide the the following scopes.
    • crm.objects.users.read
    • settings.users.teams.read
    • settings.users.read
    • crm.objects.owners.read
  7. Click Create app.

  8. Click on Continue creating.

  9. Copy the token and retain for use with G&L.
